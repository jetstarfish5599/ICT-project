The Last of Us

Game of the Year 2013

About the Game

Release Year: 2013

Developer: Naughty Dog

Publisher: Sony Computer Entertainment

Description: The Last of Us is an emotional post-apocalyptic journey that explores the bond between Joel and Ellie as they navigate a world ravaged by a fungal infection. It blends survival, stealth, and action gameplay with a compelling narrative.

Achievements

  • Winner of over 200 Game of the Year awards
  • Praised for its narrative, characters, and voice acting
  • Redefined storytelling in video games
