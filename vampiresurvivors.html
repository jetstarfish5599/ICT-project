Vampire Survivors

Game of the Year 2022

About the Game

Release Year: 2022

Developer: Luca Galante (Poncle)

Publisher: Poncle

Description: Vampire Survivors is an indie survival game with minimalist gameplay and a focus on addictive mechanics. Players fend off waves of enemies while collecting upgrades and evolving their character.

Achievements

  • Lauded for its addictive gameplay and innovative simplicity
  • Massive success as an indie title
  • Recognized for redefining survival-based games
