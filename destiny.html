Destiny

Game of the Year 2014

About the Game

Release Year: 2014

Developer: Bungie

Publisher: Activision

Description: Destiny is a shared-world first-person shooter that combines sci-fi lore, intense combat, and addictive multiplayer gameplay in a persistent online universe.

Achievements

  • Set a new standard for shared-world shooters
  • Praised for its engaging gunplay and cooperative play
  • Built a passionate community around its universe
