Dishonored

Game of the Year 2012

About the Game

Release Year: 2012

Developer: Arkane Studios

Publisher: Bethesda Softworks

Description: Dishonored is a stealth-action game that puts players in the role of Corvo Attano, a royal protector framed for murder. Set in a dystopian, steampunk world, the game offers unparalleled freedom to creatively eliminate enemies using supernatural powers and gadgets.

Achievements

  • Winner of multiple "Game of the Year" awards
  • Pioneered immersive stealth gameplay
  • Acclaimed for its unique level design and player choice
