Returnal

Game of the Year 2021

About the Game

Release Year: 2021

Developer: Housemarque

Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment

Description: Returnal is a sci-fi roguelike shooter that follows Selene, a space pilot trapped in a time loop on an alien planet. The game combines fast-paced action, challenging gameplay, and deep lore.

Achievements

  • Praised for its innovative use of roguelike mechanics
  • Exceptional visuals and atmospheric storytelling
  • Winner of Best Action Game at The Game Awards 2021
