Hades

Game of the Year 2020

About the Game

Release Year: 2020

Developer: Supergiant Games

Publisher: Supergiant Games

Description: Hades is a roguelike dungeon-crawler that combines Greek mythology, stunning visuals, and addictive gameplay. Players control Zagreus, son of Hades, as he attempts to escape the underworld.

Achievements

  • Innovative storytelling and replayability
  • Critically acclaimed for its art, music, and gameplay
  • Winner of numerous awards across categories
